Kharif Crop

The kharif crop are sown the between july to august.They are harvested at time of september to october. the major source of water is monsoon rains.

The crops that show high yield are :
Cereals :

  • Rice
  • Jowar
  • Millets
  • Maize
Fruits :
  • Apple
  • Almonds
  • Watermelon
  • Walnuts
Vegetables :
  • Chili
  • Ladiesfinger
  • Tomato
  • Bittergourd

The kharif season is best farming season for sothern part and some northern parts of India. The most common kharif crop is rice.

The kharif are some what risky because a little amount of rain at wrong time make alot of damage to crops and leads to waste of whole year.
