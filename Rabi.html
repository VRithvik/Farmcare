Rabi Crop

The rabi crops are sown in between october to december.They are harvested at time of april to june.The major source of water is rainfall

The crops that show high yield are :
Cereals :

  • Wheat
  • Barley
  • Peas
  • Gram
Fruits :
  • Banana
  • Grapes
  • Lemon
  • Orange
Vegetables :
  • Brinjal
  • Carrot
  • Broccoli
  • Capsicum

The rabi season is best farming season in northern part of India.Rabi crop is also known as winter crops as they are sown in the season of winter

and they are harvested in the season of spring.The word rabi is originated from arabic.
